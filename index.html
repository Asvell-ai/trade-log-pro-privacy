Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Trading Journal is an offline trading journal application. This app does not collect, store, or share any personal user data.

1. No Data Collection

The app does not collect personal information such as: name, email address, phone number, location, or financial account details.

2. Local Data Storage

All trade information entered in the app is stored locally on the user's device. The app does not transmit data to any server and does not use cloud storage.

3. No Third-Party Services

This app does not use advertising networks, analytics services, or third-party tracking tools.

4. Data Responsibility

Users are responsible for their data. If the app is deleted, all stored trade data may be permanently removed.

5. Changes to This Policy

If future versions of the app introduce online features or data collection, this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly.
